# Promotional Poster / Story

## Context for the AI image generator

Meowassist is a Persian/English Telegram AI bot. Key features:
- Access to 100+ AI models (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, DeepSeek, xAI)
- Crypto payments (BTC, ETH, LTC, TON, TRX, USDT)
- Iranian Rial card payments via Shaparak
- Free starter messages
- Persian-first with English support

Target audience: Iranian tech-savvy Telegram users.

## PROMPT — Feature Poster (1080×1920, portrait / Instagram story)

Top third: Meowassist cat-circuit logo (stylized cat face, one eye as chat
bubble, one as AI node) glowing with an electric cyan (#00E5FF) halo effect on
a dark purple (#6C3CE1) to dark navy (#1A1A2E) gradient background.

Middle section: 4 feature cards arranged vertically with even spacing:
  🤖 "دسترسی به بیش از ۱۰۰ مدل هوش مصنوعی" (Access to 100+ AI models)
  💳 "پرداخت با ارز دیجیتال" (Pay with crypto)
  🇮🇷 "پرداخت ریالی از طریق شاپرک" (Rial payment via Shaparak)
  🆓 "پیام‌های رایگان برای شروع" (Free messages to start)

Each card: frosted glass / glassmorphism effect, rounded corners (12px radius),
small emoji icon on the right side (RTL layout). Persian text is primary and
larger, with a smaller English subtitle underneath each line in a lighter
opacity. Cards should have a subtle white border (1px, 20% opacity).

Bottom section: "@Meowassist_Ai_bot" in white text, with a rounded button
shape underneath reading "همین الان شروع کن" (Start now) in a solid cyan
(#00E5FF) pill-shaped button with dark text.

Overall feel: modern, premium, trustworthy. Similar aesthetic to Telegram's
own promotional materials. Clean typography, plenty of breathing room between
elements.
