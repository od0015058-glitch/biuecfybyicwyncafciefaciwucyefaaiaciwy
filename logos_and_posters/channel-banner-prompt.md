# Telegram Channel Banner

## Context for the AI image generator

Meowassist is a Telegram AI bot with a cat mascot. This banner will be used as
the header image for the `@MeowAssist_Channel` Telegram channel where updates
and announcements are posted.

---

## PROMPT — Channel Banner (1280×640, landscape)

Wide banner with the Meowassist cat-circuit logo (stylized cat face with one
eye as a chat bubble, the other as a glowing AI node) centered on the image.
Background is a subtle gradient from deep purple (#6C3CE1) on the left to dark
navy (#1A1A2E) on the right. Floating around the cat: tiny, semi-transparent
icons representing the bot's features — a wallet icon, a chat bubble, crypto
coin symbols (₿ Ξ), a settings gear, a language icon (فا/EN). Very subtle,
almost like constellations or floating particles. Bottom-center text:
"meowassist.space" in small, elegant white text with slight letter-spacing.
Overall feel: premium, techy, trustworthy. No busy patterns — clean and
spacious. The logo should take up about 30% of the banner height, centered
both horizontally and vertically (slightly above center to leave room for the
URL text at bottom).
