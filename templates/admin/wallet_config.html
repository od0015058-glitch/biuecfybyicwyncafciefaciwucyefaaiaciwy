💱 USD→Toman refresher cadence

Effective interval: {{ fx_refresh_view.effective_human }} ({{ fx_refresh_view.effective }}s)  source: {{ fx_refresh_view.source }}

How often the FX refresher polls the configured upstream (FX_RATE_SOURCE) for the live USD→Toman rate. The rate snapshot powers the wallet UI's Toman display, the Custom (Toman) top-up flow, and the per-invoice locked rate at order-creation time. A shorter interval means more accurate rates (and more upstream calls — be careful with rate-limited APIs); a longer interval means the wallet may render "(approx)" markers for users.
