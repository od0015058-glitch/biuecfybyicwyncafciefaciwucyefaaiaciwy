How to read these numbers

  • Gateway revenue excludes admin and gift top-ups (manual credits and gift-code redemptions are free credit, not earnings). Same filter the dashboard "Total revenue" tile uses.
  • Wallet charges is the sum of cost_deducted_usd from usage_logs — i.e. what we charged users per token, with the markup baked in.
  • Implied OpenRouter cost is wallet_charges ÷ markup. We don't store per-call OpenRouter cost, so this is a derived figure that assumes the historical rows were charged at today's markup. If you've changed COST_MARKUP recently the lifetime number drifts; the rolling-window numbers are more reliable. The Markup eras table above re-attributes historical rows to their own era's markup — use that for "did the markup change pay for itself?" questions.
  • Net profit assumes every dollar credited will eventually be consumed at today's markup. A user who topped up $20 and never sent a prompt counts as $20 of revenue and $0 of OpenRouter cost. Treat net as "what would be left if every wallet drained tomorrow", not as realised profit.
