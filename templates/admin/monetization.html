How this is computed

  • Revenue: SUM(amount_usd_credited) over transactions with status IN ('SUCCESS', 'PARTIAL') and gateway NOT IN ('admin', 'gift'). Internal ledger adjustments (admin credit, gift codes) are excluded — they aren't real revenue.
  • Refunds: same filter but status = 'REFUNDED'. Reversals of real top-ups via the admin panel.
  • Gross AI charge: SUM(cost_deducted_usd) over usage_logs. Every call to OpenRouter logs its marked-up cost; this is what users actually paid for AI in the window.
  • OpenRouter cost: gross AI charge ÷ COST_MARKUP. The schema doesn't persist a per-row OpenRouter cost yet (next slice), so this is the operator's best current estimate.
  • Gross profit: revenue − refunds − OpenRouter cost. Excludes infra (server, IP, gateway fees) and tax — those aren't tracked in this DB.
